About Us

Om Ingole, the founder of Oppidan India, is a beacon of inspiration for the youth of today. His entrepreneurial journey began at a tender age, driven by a passion for innovation, education, and community development. Through Oppidan India, Om aims to revolutionize the education sector, empower individuals with essential skills, and foster economic growth. His story is a testament to resilience, determination, and the transformative power of entrepreneurship.

Background

Born with a natural flair for leadership and a passion for karate, Om's journey towards entrepreneurship was paved with discipline, perseverance, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. His early experiences in sports instilled in him values of resilience, teamwork, and community engagement, shaping his character and influencing his approach to challenges.

Professional Journey

At the young age of 18, Om embarked on his entrepreneurial journey with the establishment of Oppidan India in January 2024. Driven by a deep-seated desire to make a meaningful impact on society, he leveraged his skills and resources to create opportunities for growth and development. Through Oppidan India, Om aims to address critical needs in the technology sector, promote innovation, and foster community engagement.
